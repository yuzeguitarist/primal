About

Primal Guitar Quartet was founded by Yuze Pan, Jiaqi Ma, Bihan Ren, and Guanru Li on July 28, 2025. The group held its debut performance at the SOTA Concert Hall in Singapore.

Through our performances, we connect with audiences around the world, sharing the passion and artistry of classical guitar music. Each concert is a unique dialogue between performers and listeners, where musical expression transcends words and creates lasting memories. We believe in the power of live music to touch hearts and inspire souls.

Our Journey

July 28, 2025

The founding of Primal Guitar Quartet. Four passionate musicians came together with a shared vision of creating something beautiful through music.

Our first performance at the prestigious SOTA Concert Hall in Singapore, marking the beginning of our musical journey. We performed Roland Dyens Hamsa I-III.

Second Performance in Beijing

Completed our second performance in Beijing, featuring Roland Dyens Hamsa I-III and Andrew York Quiccan, expanding our repertoire and reaching new audiences.
